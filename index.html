OSMLY

OSMLY is a simple importer for OpenStreetMap. It makes importing basic features very simple by presenting them one-by-one, allowing you to make any necessary adjustment to positions or tags, flagging problems, or uploading directly to OSM. Other users can also edit simultaneously from the same pool of features. The aim is to make importing simple features quick and easy.
